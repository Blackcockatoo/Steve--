## Core Philosophy

> **"A freemium companion app dies the moment free users feel emotionally punished."**

Your pet's love, personality, and core bond are **never** paywalled. What you monetize is:
- **Expansion** (more companions, more evolution stages)
- **Depth** (advanced readouts, genome lab, full emotional spectrum)
- **Delight** (cosmetics, sound packs, visual effects)
- **Convenience** (cloud sync, multi-device, backups)
